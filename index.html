wireless is RFMi.

Building on four decades of experience in the design and manufacture of low-power radio frequency (RF) products, today RFMi is enabling OEM design engineers to connect and network more devices, equipment, and processes than ever before.

RFMi designs, develops, markets a broad range of radio frequency (RF) component and module products for low power and wireless communications. RFMi carry 5 major timing and frequency products lines for the emerging wireless applications.

  • SAW resonators, filters
  • Crystal Oscillators, TCXO, VCXO, OCXO
  • Dielectric filters
  • Patch antennas and Chip antennas
  • Certain front-end modules including LNA and SAW filters

Depth.

Founded in 1979 to design and manufacture low-power RF products using surface acoustic wave ( SAW ) technology , RFM products now include RF communications components, subsystem short-range radios , RF modules and boxed radios for a broad range of industrial, commercial and consumer applications.

Breadth.

With the acquisition of modules manufacturer Coronet in 2006 , RFM’s product portfolio provides a comprehensive selection of wireless standards and proprietary-based communications and networking products.

M2M.

Wireless technology is the key to machine-to-machine(M2M) connectivity. When embedded into original equipment manufacturers (OEM) next-generation products, RFM products enable end-users to connect, communicate, control and monitor wireless-enabled OEM devices over the Internet.
